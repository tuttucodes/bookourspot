# BookOurSpot: Project Roadmap & Screen List **Target Market:** Malaysia (Beauty & Wellness) **Reference:** Fresha ## Phase 1: User App (The Customer Experience) Focuses on discovery, booking, and managing appointments. 1. **Home/Discovery:** Search by treatment (hair, nails, massage), location-based results, trending services in Malaysia, and "Recommended for You." 2. **Search Results:** Filterable list/map view of salons with ratings, pricing, and distance. 3. **Venue Profile:** Detailed view of a salon—services menu, staff profiles, gallery, reviews, and "Book Now" CTA. 4. **Booking Flow:** Select service -> Choose professional -> Select Date/Time slot. 5. **Checkout & Payment:** Summary of booking, loyalty points (localized for MY), and payment options (GrabPay, TouchnGo, Credit Card). 6. **My Appointments:** Upcoming and past bookings, with easy "Reschedule" or "Rebook" options. 7. **User Profile:** Settings, saved venues, and payment methods. ## Phase 2: Business App (Admin & Management) Focuses on business operations, staff management, and growth. 8. **Dashboard:** Daily overview—today's appointments, revenue stats, and quick actions. 9. **Calendar/Schedule:** Main working view—drag-and-drop appointment management for all staff members. 10. **Client Database:** List of customers with booking history, notes, and contact info. 11. **Services & Pricing:** Management of the service menu, durations, and categories. 12. **Staff Management:** Rosters, commissions, and individual performance tracking. 13. **Marketing & Loyalty:** Tools to create promos, blast SMS/Email, and manage a loyalty program. 14. **Settings:** Business profile, location, and operating hours. --- **Next Steps:** Once you approve this list, I will create a unified **Design System** and begin generating these screens, starting with the User App's Home and Venue Profile.
